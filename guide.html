First Aid Guide

CPR (Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation)

Check response, call emergency services, begin 30 chest compressions followed by 2 rescue breaths.

Watch CPR Tutorial

Bleeding Control

Apply direct pressure with a clean cloth. Keep the wound elevated. Do not remove embedded objects.

Watch Bleeding First Aid

Burns

Cool the burn under running water for at least 10 minutes. Do not apply ice or creams immediately.

Watch Burn First Aid

Choking

Give 5 back blows between shoulder blades and 5 abdominal thrusts. Repeat until object is removed.

Watch Choking Aid

Fractures

Immobilize the area using a splint or cloth. Avoid moving the injured limb. Seek immediate help.

Watch Fracture Handling

Shock

Lay the person down, elevate legs if possible, keep them warm and calm until help arrives.

Watch Shock First Aid

Heatstroke

Move to a cool place, remove excess clothing, and use cold compresses. Hydrate and monitor.

Watch Heatstroke Care
